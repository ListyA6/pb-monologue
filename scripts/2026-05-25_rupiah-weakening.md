# Script — Rupiah Weakening (FX as an operator problem)

**Length:** 60s · **Date:** 2026-05-25 · **Pillar:** The Operator's Lens (~20%, peer voice)

> **Calibration note:** The brand excludes Indonesia-macro hot takes. This is NOT a "here's why the rupiah is dropping" explainer. It's an *operator's* problem: my costs are in a currency that just dropped, and the equipment/ingredients I buy are priced in dollars. That lesson travels — Marcus (imports kitchen gear) and Sofia (imports linens/produce) live the same exposure. Macro is the setup; the receipt is the point.

---

## 0. Pre-Brief

- **Topic / claim:** The rupiah dropped `[FILL %]` and it quietly made my Floural launch more expensive — here's the real number.
- **The artifact / proof:** The oven quote for Floural (priced in USD / imported), shown twice — old price vs today's price after the FX move. Bonus: a supplier invoice or POS cost line.
- **The open loop:** "One number on a screen made a business 22 juta harder to start." — *which* number, *how much*, withheld until payoff.
- **The lesson / payoff line:** *"I can't control the rupiah. I can control how exposed I am to it."* (written first — everything builds backward to this)
- **Target length:** 60s
- **Word count target:** 130–145
- **Language:** English VO, Indonesian SRT track uploaded separately. Numbers burned on-screen in both (`Rp22jt ≈ $1.3K`).

**BACKUP: H10 (before/after)** — pure visual delta, near-silent, strongest for A/B if H7 underperforms.
